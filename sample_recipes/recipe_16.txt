1. **Banana Oatmeal Bowl**
2. **Preparation Time**: 5 minutes
3. **Cooking Time**: 10 minutes
4. **Number of Servings**: 1
5. **Ingredients**:
   - 1 ripe banana
   - 1/2 cup rolled oats
   - 1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
   - 1 tablespoon honey
   - 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
   - A pinch of salt
6. **Instructions**:
   1. In a small saucepan, combine the oats, milk, and a pinch of salt. Bring to a gentle simmer over medium heat.
   2. Cook the oatmeal, stirring occasionally, until the milk is absorbed and the oats are tender, about 5-7 minutes.
   3. Meanwhile, slice the banana into thin rounds.
   4. Once the oatmeal is cooked, remove it from the heat and stir in the honey and cinnamon.
   5. Transfer the oatmeal to a bowl and top with the sliced banana.
   6. Enjoy it as a delicious and healthy breakfast or snack!
7. **Serving Suggestion**:
Serve this hearty and healthy banana oatmeal bowl as a nutritious breakfast or a filling afternoon snack. For a heartier meal, you can also top it with some chopped nuts or a dollop of plain yogurt.
