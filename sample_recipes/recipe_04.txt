1. **Spinach and Egg Bake**

2. **Preparation Time**: 15 minutes 
3. **Cooking Time**: 30 minutes 
4. **Number of Servings**: 2 
5. **Ingredients**:
   - 4 large eggs
   - 1/2 cup milk
   - 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or mozzarella work well)
   - 2 cups baby spinach, chopped
   - 1/4 teaspoon salt
   - 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
   - 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano

6. **Instructions**:
   1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease a small baking dish or two individual ramekins.
   2. In a bowl, beat the eggs and then add milk, salt, pepper, and oregano. Beat until well combined.
   3. Stir in the cheese and chopped spinach.
   4. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish or ramekins.
   5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the egg mixture is set and lightly browned.
   6. Remove from the oven and let it cool down for a few minutes before serving.

7. **Serving Suggestions**:
   - Serve this tasty egg bake with some toasted bread and a side salad for a complete meal. It's also great on its own as a quick and nutritious breakfast or brunch option.
